Fynder is a delightfully simple way to manage classes and appointments online.  We know you’re busy, so we’ve made it easier than ever to create schedules, take bookings, get paid and better engage your customers with messages that matter to them.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `fynder.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Create a Fynder account at https://fynder.io
1. Navigate to Settings -> Fynder in your WordPress admin
1. Copy your business ID (found from your "Account Settings" in https://admin.fynder.io) into the "Business ID" field
1. Whenever you want to link to your class schedule, use the shortcode `[booking-button]View Schedule[/booking-button]`.
